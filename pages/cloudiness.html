Cloudiness

Here we see that the measurement of cloudiness is possibly not exact as the measurements greatly group towards the marks divisible by 10. Cloudiness does not appear to correlate to latitude other than we see that just north of the equator there are few cities with a zero cloudiness rating. It appears from this data that latitude is minimally affected by latitude directly.
